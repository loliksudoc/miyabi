BIOGRAPHY

Miyabi Hoshimi is the youngest Void Hunter in New Eridu and the commander of Section 6. She hails from the prestigious Hoshimi Clan, renowned for their martial arts mastery. Despite her status, she focuses on field operations rather than administrative tasks. Wielding the cursed katana "Tailless," Miyabi combines traditional combat techniques with unique abilities, maintaining order and confronting corruption in New Eridu.
